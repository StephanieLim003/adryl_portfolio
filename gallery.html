Write Up

In 2018 I got a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to represent my school by participating in the National Day Parade (NDP) as the show choir, where we brought back the old tradition of the show choir in NDP. In addition, I volunteered to be a Peer Supported Leader to learn how to care for others in times of need and be a team player with group projects to support our peers. In 2019, I advanced from Normal Academic Levels to Express. Our choir attended Zarathustra at Esplanade concert hall, where we performed Belshazzar’s Feast with other choir professionals. In the same year, and was promoted to an Executive Committee in the Peer Support Board. I learned to be a reliable leader and a better follower there. As well as in 2019’s Bright Spot challenge as the leader. We went to an old folk’s home to entertain the elderlies with the games we made together.
